# iOS Developer Section ā€” Design

**Date:** 2026-02-28
**Status:** Approved

## Overview

Add a dedicated iOS developer section to eof.news, following the hybrid pattern:
- A standalone `/ios/` tracker page (like `/github/`, `/outages/`)
- iOS as a 6th news niche in the existing daily pipeline

## Goals

Serve iOS developers with curated Apple platform news, Swift/SwiftUI content, App Store updates, and community blog posts ā€” even though the site owner is not an iOS developer.

## Architecture

### 1. `ios.py` ā€” New dedicated tracker script

Fetches from curated iOS/Apple RSS feeds, generates JSON + page.

**Input feeds:**
- Swift Blog: `https://swift.org/blog/feed.xml`
- Apple Developer News: `https://developer.apple.com/news/rss/news.rss`
- NSHipster: `https://nshipster.com/feed.xml`
- Hacking with Swift: `https://www.hackingwithswift.com/articles/rss`
- iOS Dev Weekly: `https://iosdevweekly.com/issues.rss`
- Swift by Sundell: `https://swiftbysundell.com/feed.rss`

**Output:**
- `docs/_data/ios.json` ā€” structured feed data (spotlight + sections)
- `docs/ios/index.md` ā€” rendered `/ios/` page

**Page layout:**
- Spotlight card ā€” latest Swift or Xcode release (like Repo of the Day)
- Apple Developer News section ā€” last 5 items from official Apple feed
- Community section ā€” last 10 items from NSHipster, Hacking with Swift, Swift by Sundell, iOS Dev Weekly
- Timestamp footer
